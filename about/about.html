About Us


About Intern Board

Welcome to Intern Board, the online platform connecting students and recent graduates with companies and organizations offering internship opportunities. Our mission is to help bridge the gap between education and employment by providing access to valuable work experience and networking opportunities for young professionals.

Our Story

Intern Board was founded in 2023 by a group of former students who saw a need for a more streamlined and efficient way for students and companies to connect for internships. As recent graduates themselves, they understood the challenges of finding internships and gaining the experience necessary to kickstart their careers. They wanted to create a platform that would make the internship search process easier and more accessible for everyone.

Our Team

At Intern Board, we're passionate about helping students and recent graduates succeed. Our team is made up of experienced professionals from a variety of backgrounds, including recruitment, technology, and education. We work tirelessly to provide the best possible experience for both our student users and our company partners.

Our Values

At Intern Board, we believe in: Providing equal opportunities for all students, regardless of background or experience level. Supporting diversity and inclusion in the workplace. Building strong relationships with our partner companies based on trust, transparency, and mutual benefit. Creating a user-friendly and secure platform that prioritizes user privacy and data protection.
